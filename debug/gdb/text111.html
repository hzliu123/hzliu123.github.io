Tracepoint Restrictions

  • Tracepoint expressions are intended to gather objects (lvalues)

    • You cannot call functions, access to convenience variables or modify values (except trace state variables)

  • Collection of local variables during while-stepping may behave erratically

    • The stepping action may enter a new scope or the location of the variable may change

  • Stack is not collected automatically

    • collect *(char *)$esp@300 for backtrace command
